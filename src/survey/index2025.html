Unlock NYC

Survey 2025

Who is Unlock NYC?

Unlock NYC is a fair housing nonprofit led by women of color who have faced housing discrimination due to government rental assistance. We create tools to help tenants fight source of income (SOI) discrimination and secure stable housing.

What is this survey about?

We are working with a committee of New Yorkers impacted by homelessness and housing discrimination to study how race, gender, and other factors affect voucher holders’ experiences with landlords, brokers, and city agencies like the Human Resources Administration (HRA).

What will I be asked to do?

This anonymous survey takes about 15 minutes. It includes questions about your demographic background and experiences with HRA, landlords, and brokers.

Am I required to participate?

No, you do not have to complete this survey. Your participation is voluntary. Your ability to use Unlock NYC’s Rights Recorder mobile app or any of our other programs and services will not be affected, whether you participate or not.

How will my information be used?

We will analyze survey responses to highlight challenges voucher holders face and recommend policy changes.

Will my participation be confidential?

Yes, the survey is anonymous. We analyze responses in aggregate (e.g., “50% of participants reported unresponsive landlords”) and do not share individual answers.

How is my information stored and protected?

Your survey is temporarily linked to your phone number for gift card distribution. After claiming your gift, we delete your phone number. Data is securely stored on an encrypted, password-protected platform. Only our research team can access it.

Are there risks to sharing my information?

While we take strong security measures, there is a small risk of unauthorized access. To reduce this, survey data is kept separate from user profiles, and phone numbers are deleted once gift cards are claimed.

What are the benefits?

You’ll receive a $15 gift card and may qualify for a paid focus group. Your input helps us advocate for a fairer voucher system.

Can I withdraw my information later?

Yes, you can contact us to revoke your consent. However, once your phone number is removed, we won’t be able to trace your survey.

Questions or concerns?

Contact Manon Vergerio, Head of Data & Advocacy, at manon@weunlock.nyc
